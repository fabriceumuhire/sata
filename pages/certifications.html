Certifications & Compliance

Certification is the core task of SATA, this includes defining, issuing and monitoring of Certificate of Compliance to private and public entities.

The certification process is a combination of Trust Assurance Mechanisms which defines the flow, responsabilities and methods to ensure compliance with defined Level Of Assurance by issuing a Certificate of Compliance to a Service Provider and/or Identity Provider.

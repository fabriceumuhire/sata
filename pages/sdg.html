SMART AFRICA TRUST ALLIANCE - SATA Benefits& SDGs

SATA's benefits

Considering marginalised people as well as economic actors who cannot prove their identities across africa, SATA enables socio-economic inclusion for all by providing a safe and secure way to access different services across all Smart Africa Member States.


SATA to empower better regulations and services

SATA addresses these and more identification cross border related issues, all Smart Africa Member States benefit new possibilities for reliable and trusted cross border exchanges which include movement of people, movement of money, movement goods and movement of data.


Private sector benefits

  • Penetration of cross border payment transactions from traders who are currently without legal identities.
  • Business expansion through One Time Business Registration across the continent.
  • SATA enables international businesses to claim relief of double taxation which will boost their income.
  • Improved services through better processes, enhanced service offerings which will drive customer loyalty.

Public & Social benefits

  • Accelerate digital inclusion for families and marginalized groups to cross border services.
  • Access to healthcare and public services particularly for women in low-income countries without legal identities.
  • Inclusion in cross borders formal trading which was limited by lack of digital identities.

SDGs

SATA revolutionizes access to public and private services and anchor the foundations of a vibrant digital economy in Africa. This helps develop socio-economic benefits to all by accelerating inclusion and access whereby supporting the SDGs and stimulate the growth of intra-african trade.

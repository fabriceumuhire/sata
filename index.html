What is SATA?

Smart Africa Trust Alliance (SATA) is a continental consortium of both public and private members which aims to mobilize Africa to its full potential into a single digital market while respecting the sovereignty of African countries. SATA provides certifications to ID and service providers and offers a federated platform to share information under data sharing rules mutually agreed by African states. SATA is a mechanism of cooperation to ensure harmonized policies are followed and allow transfer of data across countries in Africa.

With more than 41% of the African population without legal identity and intra-Africa trade accounted for less than 20% of Africa's total trade, SATA includes and empowers everyone to be part of the transformation of Africa into a single digital market.

What is SATA?

Smart Africa Trust Alliance (SATA) is a continental consortium which aims to empower people and organizations to interact and transact across the borders of Smart Africa State Members through a digital framework.

“Digital Identity systems are at the heart of Africa’s digital transformation agenda. They can help us reach marginalized populations, enhance public service delivery, and unlock new economic opportunities”

Mr. Lacina KONÉ
Director General / CEO of Smart Africa

With more than 41% of population without legal identity, SATA will facilitate to empower these people to engage across the continent through integration of functional solutions with Service Providers and create confidence to new opportunities for transnational traders easing the present border barriers.

Welcome to SATA

Smart Africa Trust Alliance is a continental platform of public and private institutions joining the interoperability framework of the national data registers and identities.

Smart Africa Trust Alliance initiates and supports the cross-border services to citizens and institutions based on mutually agreed standards by African governments and relevant organizations to provide trust services.

“Digital Identity systems are at the heart of Africa’s digital transformation agenda. They can help us reach marginalized populations, enhance public service delivery, and unlock new economic opportunities”

Mr. Lacina KONÉ
Director General / CEO of Smart Africa

How SATA works

The Mission behind SATA is to realize the African Single Digital Market with interoperability of the National Data Registers or information systems.

A platform of cooperation to ensure harmonized policies, standards, and requirements are created and joined. SATA will enable the interoperability of data exchange, identities, and services across countries in Africa.

SATA is an “Armed Wing” of Smart Africa to effectively build a Single Digital Market

SATA includes and empowers everyone to be part of the transformation of Africa into a Single Digital Market.

SATA creates a framework that enables the trusted and effective movement of people, data, services and goods to stimulate the growth of intra-African trade while support the African Continental Free Trade Area.
